About Dr. Pams

Leadership is not a title. It is the day-to-day discipline of lifting people, protecting them, and helping them grow.

Dr. Fredlan “Pams” Mpama is an author, speaker, leadership educator, consultant, and AHA Instructor who bridges academic rigor with real-world experience. His professional journey spans meteorology, environmental health and safety, human resources, and executive leadership across complex, highly regulated industries.

He holds a PhD in Leadership (Organizational Leadership), an MBA in Global Operations, and a BA in Mathematics. Dr. Pams has led and supported safety and HR initiatives on major projects and in organizations ranging from energy and construction to higher education and technology.

At the core of his work is a simple conviction: when you develop people’s skills, protect their safety, and coach their hearts, performance improves naturally. His research and practice focus on emotional intelligence, transformational leadership, psychological safety, and human performance.
